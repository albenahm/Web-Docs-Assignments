of www.breadandbutter.cooking

Home Page home Display text about the bakery and its brief history It is the landing page of the website for the bakery. It takes to the required pages with the huperlinks provided in the hyperlink
Menus menu Displaies the available food products in the bakery. It has the link to its 4 sub pages, namely Cakes and Pastries, Biscuits, Chocolate and Vegan. It displaies the product-list.xml as one of its content.
